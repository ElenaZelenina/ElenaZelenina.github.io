About Us

We are a interior design service that focus on what's best for your home and what's best for you!

How is it that in 2019 houses are still being built with the same old dull designs and every room is being decorated with the same “assemble yourself” IKEA furniture. With the levels of technology that people use daily, from wearable tech, to refrigerators that automatically add items to your shopping list, the public is yearning for something new and groundbreaking. Perhaps lights and shades that sense when it’s movie time, or furniture that can help wake you up on schedule. Our company offers that “Intelligent Design.” In an innovative shift, we allow customers to place an order and customize a couch, interactive appliance, thermostat, powered shades, lighting, all the above, or something completely different and request it to do and communicate in endless ways. Unlike other furniture stores, we don’t tell you what you need, instead we want to bring to life what you have only imagined. 21st Century ingenuity with the quality and craftsmanship of the premodern era. Come meet with our designers today, the only thing you need to bring is your imagination, we will take care of the rest. At “Intelligent Design” we want to help you build the future.
